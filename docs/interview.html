Interview with Nicole Conroy
By Colin O'Donnell

Partially Deaf, Her Stories Fully Told: Ms. Conroy’s Journey

Her matte-black hair droops over her shoulders, and three scars mark her pale-skinned figure, with only one ear that picks up the world’s sounds. Yet on Wednesday, on campus, she laughs freely, softly but energetically recounting her stories about sorority life, road trips across the country and her childhood pool parties in Alabama. Her memories reveal the resilience that guided her throughout college and shaped her into the person she is today.

Nicole Conroy has grown up chasing nature. Her early years were a combination of Hawaii, Washington, Alabama, Colorado and Maryland. Each place molded her in small and significant ways, she said. Road trips around the states transformed into her favorite childhood memories. The wilderness of Wyoming, the sunshine of Florida and the deserts of Arizona left a lasting impact on her love of travel and exploration. Apart from moving often, which might be detrimental for other children, Nicole learned to be strong through new scenery. These qualities that she would carry to future chapters of her life. ​College marked both an influential but also troublesome part of her life.

It brought many challenges that she did not expect. “My freshman year was rough, I was struggling academically and socially,” she quietly admits, “but then I joined Alpha Sigma Tau sorority and got less lonely, and I began to find methods for studying and got accommodations which really improved my college experience.” Now, Nicole uses her time apart from classes to enjoy events with her sorority, which include philanthropic events, yoga exercises and building puzzles.

​Nicole’s major, communications, began as a “safe choice,” but eventually grew into a passion. “A lot of things I do in my personal life require creativity and communication,” she mentioned. Her favorite classes this semester are English 202D and cycling. She is in her junior year and on her way to graduate from Penn State Behrend soon. Her vision is set on social media marketing and networking, a field where her creativity can thrive.

​Nicole Conroy’s journey has been shaped by constant change and various setbacks. Conroy continues to push forward through life with the same resilience reflected in her childhood tales. Whether it’s traveling across the country, creating connections in her sorority or preparing for her career in communications, she embraces each step with confidence and determination, and her story is far from over.
